# Sending HTML Emails with Python
#### --> Go to `html_emails.py` file.
####  --> Replace the `email_id` and `pswd` variable with your sender's Email and Password.
####  --> Replace the `names` and `emails` variable with your recipients emails and names in the form of list.
####  --> Replace `html` variable's content with your html.
####  --> Replace `msg[Subject]` with your email subject.
####  --> Replace the `fp1, fp2, fp3` variables with your logo address if you want to add link to your fb/insta pages in footer. 
####  --> Save and run the file, if the mail is sent successfully, it prints email sent successfully.
